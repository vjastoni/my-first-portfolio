Mobile Application: Diaeta

Diaeta is a simple, user-friendly weight loss app that focuses on calorie monitoring and weight tracking. It is a health care application for dietary plans and healthy lifestyle. Diaeta calculates your daily calorie needs and creates a unique weight loss strategy based on your weight, age, and health goals. This application was developed by our team in 2019 when we are practicing for cluster division of Tagisan ng Talino at STI College.

Restaurant Management System

A restaurant management system (RMS) is a sort of point-of-sale software intended primarily for restaurants, bars, food trucks, and other food service establishments. Unlike a point-of-sale system, RMS covers all back-end requirements, including as inventory and personnel management. This serve as our second project after the Diaeta app when we were practicing for the cluster division at STI College Tagisan ng Talino in 2019.
